A C-Arm machine is a versatile medical imaging device used primarily for real-time fluoroscopy in surgical, orthopedic, and interventional procedures. It features high-resolution imaging capabilities with both fluoroscopy and radiography modes, allowing surgeons to visualize internal structures with clarity. Modern C-Arms come equipped with either a flat panel detector (FPD) or an image intensifier, ensuring high-quality digital images while minimizing radiation exposure.
